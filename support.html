Frequently Asked Questions

How often is content updated?

New AI news digests are published monthly.

Can I bookmark articles?

Yes. Tap the bookmark icon on any article to save it and find it later in Bookmarks.

How do I share an article?

Open an article and use the Share button to send it with your preferred apps.

Is there offline reading?

Yes. Saved digests and bookmarked articles are available without an internet connection.

Can I search by keyword?

Yes. Use the Search tab to find articles by title, summary, category, or content.

Accessibility features?

Adjust text size, use system Dark Mode, and VoiceOver is supported throughout the app.

Troubleshooting

  1. Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  2. Pull to refresh on the Magazines list.
  3. Make sure you’re on the latest app version from the App Store.
  4. Restart your device.
  5. Reinstall the app (your bookmarks are stored on-device).

If issues continue, email terrion.services@gmail.com with screenshots if possible.
